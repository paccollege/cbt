PAC COLLEGE EGBEADA: Shaping the Future

Excellence in Education, Integrity in Character.

Our Vision

To be the premier secondary institution in Lagos, recognized globally for producing students of exceptional academic prowess, moral integrity, and entrepreneurial zeal, ready to excel in a rapidly changing world.

Our Mission

To provide a rigorous, balanced, and student-centered learning environment that nurtures critical thinking, encourages intellectual curiosity, and instills the values of discipline, respect, and community service in every student.

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1995 in the heart of Egbeda, PAC COLLEGE has grown from a modest institution to a beacon of academic excellence. Our commitment to utilizing modern teaching methodologies, including this advanced CBT platform, ensures that our students are well-prepared for both local and international examinations.

We pride ourselves on our highly qualified faculty, state-of-the-art laboratories, and a holistic approach to education that prioritizes both mental acuity and personal development. PAC College is more than a school; it's a foundation for lifelong success.
